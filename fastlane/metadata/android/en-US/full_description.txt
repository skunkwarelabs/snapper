Snapper is an offline-friendly fishing companion.

* Log your catches with a photo, species, length/weight, time and GPS location.
* Identify fish from a photo with an on-device classifier — no network needed.
* Find fishable water near you on an OpenStreetMap map, with rivers, lakes and streams highlighted.
* Look up bag and size limits for all 50 US states plus D.C., and see which species are stocked where.
* Save and revisit your favorite fishing spots.

All your data stays on your device — no account, no servers, no tracking, no ads.
